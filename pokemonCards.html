2020 Pokemon JPN.SWSH Galarian Sirfetch’d V Amazing Volt Tackle - FA #106
Galarian Sirfetch’dV: ガラルネギガナイト (V Galarian NegigaknightV)
is a Fighting-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as one of the SWSH Black Star Promos. In Japan, this card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Amazing Volt Tackle expansion.
2000 Pokemon Rocket Dark Gyarados – Holo Prerelease #8 PSA 8 NM – MT
Pokémon Power: Final Beam ファイナルビーム
When Dark Gyarados is Knocked Out by an attack, flip a coin. If heads, this power does 20 damage for each Water Energy attached to Dark Gyarados to the Pokémon that Knocked Out Dark Gyarados. This power doesn’t work if Dark Gyarados is Asleep, Confused, or Paralyzed.
2000 Pokemon Rocket Dark Alakazam 1st Edition #18 PSA 3 VG
Dark Alakazam (Japanese: わるいフーディン Bad Foodin) is a Psychic-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Team Rocket expansion. This card was included in the Team Rocket expansion as both a Holofoil and Non Holofoil card, first released in the Japanese Rocket Gang expansion as a Holofoil card.
ACE Grading 7 NM 2000 Pokemon Rocket 1st Edition #50 Charmander
Charmander (Japanese: ヒトカゲ Hitokage) is a Fire-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Team Rocket expansion.

1999 Pokemon Game Bulbasaur #44 PSA 8 NM-MT
